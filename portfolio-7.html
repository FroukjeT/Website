Module project

Engineering

In module 7 of Creative Technology, students learn about entrepreneurship and starting their own startup company. In the module project, students work together in teams to come up with their own startup.

  For our own little startup we made a 'smart lighter'. This lighter helps you to stop smoking in a different way than you might have already tried before. Instead of stopping with smoking trough nicotine patches/gum, or stopping cold turkey, we thought of a way where you can reduce your smoking habit. The idea is that a user will fill in their daily habits regarding to smoking, and the program will set up a plan for them to stop smoking. For example, if someone start with 10 cigarretes a day, the program will give them 10 times to use their lighter. After using the lighter 10 times, the user cannot use their lighter anymore, so it becomes impossible (or at least way harder) to light up a cigarette.

  By getting used to the habit of not smoking anymore slowly, we believe this can be really effective. The lighter is also connected to an app, where you can see your statistics/progress and can also track other people/friends.

Literature Research

Research

Next to entrepreneurship, this module focussed on ethics regarding technology and businesses. 

 In these pictures an example can be seen of one of the topics that has been discussed. It is about the conflict between Apple and the American FBI, where there was a prisoner who had important information on his phone, but the phone was protected by a password. The FBI asked Apple to open a back door and break into the phone of the prisoner. Apple refused to do this, giving arguments such as 'if we do this now, who says where it stops' and 'this is a core value of our business model, protection'. What is the right ethical decision to make?

 The other topics we included were: introduction to acting responsibly, philosophy of technology, ethics of design, politics of design, ethics of privacy, professional code of ethics & sustainability and business ethics.
