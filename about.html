About me


Welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Froukje Temme, I'm 22 years old and I recently graduated from the Creative Technology program at the University of Twente (October 2022). Currently I’m looking for a job to gain work experience before pursuing with a master. My interests are travelling, sports and technology, and I’m fascinated by new technology and the smart world trough sensors, actuators and virtual reality. My goal is to make a difference by means of technology, connecting the perfect elements and people together. I’m interested in the fields of health, revalidation and sport, together with smart technology and lifestyle technology.


Originally I'm from Breda, a city in the south of the Netherlands, close to the Belgium border. I have lived there almost my whole life, as I went to elementary and high school there. After graduating from high school, I decided to take a gap year and went to Canada to study a year abroad. During this time I took courses such as tourism, sociology and electronics and further developed myself as a person. In 2019 I started the studies Creative Technology at the university of Twente, where I chose the direction smart technology in the second year and had the possibility to go abroad again in the third year. In the third year I went abroad to Sweden, Umea University. I followed health psychology, cognitive psychology and interaction technology. I believe my experiences abroad have helped me in developing myself and are useful for my future career.

Please have a look at my portfolio, and if there are any questions don’t hesitate to ask!
