Human Centered Design

Module project

In this module, we were introduced to a real company and a client for which we had to come up with a solution for their problem. Our client was a veterenarian, who had the problem that his cats and dogs see each other in the waiting room, whereafter cats would get stressed out.

As a solution, we designed a wall that the client can place in the waiting room, as a distinction between the cats and the dogs. Not only are the cats and the dogs unable to see each other anymore, but the wall also has a lot of technological features to keep the cats relaxed. The user can put the cat's travel box with the cat inside the wall, and in the wall there are various options. Temperature change, sound, light and smell can be chosen (or pre-settings), in order to keep the cat relaxed.
